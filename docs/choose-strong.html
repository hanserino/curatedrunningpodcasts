Choose Strong

The host, Sally McRae, is a pro athlete , wife, mother, and biz owner. She seeks to encourage others to live a strong and courageous life and covers a variety of life focused topics including running, total wellness, mindset, parenting, life balance, the impact of social media, and staying motivated while working toward goals.
